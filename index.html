    About

    The Connecticut Town Profiles is a project of CTData Collaborative in partnership with AdvanceCT (formerly the Connecticut Economic Resource Center).

    The Connecticut Town Profiles are two-page reports of demographic and economic information for Connecticut's municipalities, regions, and the state as a whole. They contain information about population, major employers, education, fiscal information, labor force, housing and quality of life.

    These town profiles are the property of AdvanceCT. They can be used free of charge by external organizations, as long as AdvanceCT and CTData Collaborative are cited.
